SOCIAL ECONOMIC ACCELERATOR LAB (SEAL)

Accelerating Digital Transformation in Indonesia

Singhasari SEZ-AWS Economic Acceleratol Lab (SEAL) is a strategic business development initiative between Singhasari Special Economic Zone (Singhasari SEZ) and Amazon Web Services. This initiative is supported by the Government of Indonesia, Singhasari SEZ as the first SEZ in digital technology, supported by AWS, share similar vision for Indonesia, which is to accelerate digital technology adoption across various sectors
